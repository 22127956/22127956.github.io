Services

Mobile World Station provides a range of in-store services for purchasing, repairing, and troubleshooting mobile devices. In addition to helping you buy a phone and choose a network plan, staff offer expert advice and support to get your device set up and running

Purchases and upgrades

New phones: Staff can guide you through the process of selecting a new device and choosing between major brands like Apple, Samsung, and Google. This is often paired with setting up a new pay-monthly or prepaid contract plan.
Upgrades: When your contract is nearing its end, a shop can help you choose and set up an upgrade, sometimes with special discounts.
SIM-only plans: If you are happy with your current phone, you can purchase a new SIM-only plan to get a new deal on minutes, texts, and data.

In-store setup and data transfer

"Walk Out Working" service: This allows you to have your new phone set up with your account and information before you leave the store.
Data transfer: Technicians can help migrate your contacts, photos, videos, and apps from your old device to your new one.
Email and app setup: Experts can help configure your email and other essential applications on your new device.

Repair and technical support

Repairs: Many common problems can be fixed in-store, often on the same day. Typical repairs include:
Screen replacements for cracks and responsiveness issues
Battery replacements for poor charging or battery life
Charging port repairs for charging problems
Water damage repair to salvage a phone after exposure to liquid
Software troubleshooting: For performance issues or application errors, technicians can diagnose and fix software problems.
Diagnostics: Some shops will perform a free or low-cost diagnostic service to determine the problem before proceeding with a repair.
Unlocking: Shops can offer services to unlock your phone so it can be used on different network carriers.
